# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Product Owner

### Role Summary
Champions project vision and objectives; acts as the primary voice of the customer and key stakeholders. Works with Product Managers and Project Managers to convert business goals into actionable items.

### Responsibilities
- Owns and communicates product vision to the team
- Prioritizes work to maximize business value
- Clarifies requirements and answers questions from the team
- Engages regularly with stakeholders to gather feedback

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Managers on roadmap and backlog
- Coordinates with Project Managers on planning and prioritization
- Works with Developers and Technical Leads to clarify and refine deliverables

---

## Quality Advocate

### Role Summary
Ensures product quality standards are defined, understood, and maintained throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Defines and communicates quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Reviews deliverables and provides quality feedback
- Identifies and escalates quality risks
- Facilitates continuous improvement in testing and verification practices

### Interactions
- Works closely with Developers on test coverage and defect resolution
- Advises Project Managers on quality risks and status
- Partners with Product Owners to clarify acceptance criteria

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Owns technical architecture and design decisions; mentors developers and bridges between engineering and other functions.

### Responsibilities
- Leads technical decision-making and design reviews
- Ensures code quality, maintainability, and scalability
- Mentors and supports development team members
- Translates business requirements into technical solutions

### Interactions
- Works with Developers to resolve technical challenges
- Advises Product Owners and Product Managers on technical feasibility
- Supports Project Managers in technical planning and estimation

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates communication and execution of significant process, product, or organizational changes.

### Responsibilities
- Plans and leads communication for upcoming changes
- Prepares teams and stakeholders for adoption or process shifts
- Monitors adoption and addresses resistance or challenges

### Interactions
- Works with Product Owners and Project Managers to schedule changes
- Communicates with all project stakeholders
- Collaborates with Quality Advocate to ensure change quality

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Acts as a bridge between business stakeholders and the technical team; ensures all requirements are clear, actionable, and aligned with business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Gathers, analyzes, and documents business and functional requirements
- Translates requirements into user stories or technical tasks
- Supports Product Owners and Product Managers in defining acceptance criteria

### Interactions
- Works directly with stakeholders to clarify needs
- Collaborates with Developers and Technical Lead to ensure requirements are feasible and understood
- Supports Quality Advocate in validating that requirements are met

---
